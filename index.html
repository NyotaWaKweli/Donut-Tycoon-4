🍩 About This Game

🍩 What Is This

Donut Tycoon is an idle clicker game. Click the donut to earn donuts, buy buildings that bake automatically, and watch your empire grow. Simple on the surface, but underneath there's a full game loop, state management, and persistent saves.

💾 Saving Your Progress

Use your email as a key to save and load your game. Nothing gets sent anywhere — everything stays on your device in localStorage. Different email, different save slot.

🌍 Braggers Community

Once you've built something worth flexing, share a bragger to the community gallery. There's a cooldown between shares so take your time, build something impressive, then show it off.

⚠️ Heads Up

This is NOT an idle game with offline accumulation. There is no auto-save. Save manually whenever you feel satisfied with your donuts or before closing the game. You've been warned.

📖 My Touch

The original Donut Tycoon was written by someone on SoloLearn. I found it, fixed the broken save button, added the bragger generator, the community, and a bunch of small details that make everything feel more alive. Check the My Touch page at the bottom for the full story.

💡 The Real Point

All the community stuff is fun, but the real reason I shared this code is so others can see some JavaScript basics in action. Clicker games teach you about game loops, state management, and local storage without needing a backend or libraries. Take the code. Break it. Learn from it. Make something better.

Hopefully y'all learn something ;)
